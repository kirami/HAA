Retail Location

We have a booth in the Antique Trove in Roseville, one of the largest antique malls in Northern California. If you are planning a visit, we suggest you allow several hours for browsing, as it is full of many wonderful items besides our records, phonographs and sheet music. The mall is open 7 days a week, from 10 AM to 6 PM. Visit their web site for a map and further information. We are situated on Aisle D, dealer number 250. If you can't find our booth, just ask any of the salespeople to direct you to the booth that has all the records and phonographs.

Antique Trove - 236 Harding Blvd, Roseville CA 95678
Phone (916) 786-2777
From I-80 in Roseville, take the Douglas Blvd. West off ramp, go to the first traffic light, which is Harding Blvd, turn right and drive about 1/2 mile North. The store is on the right.
