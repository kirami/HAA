We would like to tell you about a new record auction that is like nothing you have ever seen before. We have developed an entirely new format for viewing our auction catalog and placing bids online plus we've added some new features as well. With our new system you can:
  • Browse the catalog, which includes sorting and filtering options.
  • Place new bids.
